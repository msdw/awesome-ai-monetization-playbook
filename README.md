## What This Is

A structured monetization reference for AI builders. It covers:

- **17 monetization models** — from subscription SaaS to consulting retainers
- **Documented offers** — real AI service and product offers with pricing signals
- **Pricing patterns** — value-based, usage-based, tiered, and hybrid approaches
- **Distribution channels** — how to reach buyers for each model
- **Delivery models** — how to deliver AI services profitably
- **Templates** — proposal templates, pricing sheets, delivery checklists

## What This Is Not

- Not financial advice
- Not "make $10k/month with AI" content
- Not a guarantee of income
- Not a list of get-rich-quick schemes

Every offer is a hypothesis. Pricing signals are observations from public sources, not guarantees.
