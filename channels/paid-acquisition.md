# Paid Acquisition

Paid acquisition uses advertising spend to generate awareness, trials, or leads. For AI service providers, paid channels can accelerate growth when organic channels are proven but slow to scale.

## Common paid channels for AI businesses

**LinkedIn Ads**: effective for B2B services targeting specific roles and industries. High cost per click but strong targeting precision. Typical CPCs range from $6–$15 for B2B audiences.

**Google Ads**: effective for capturing buyer intent through search. Appropriate when buyers search for the specific service type. Requires keyword research to identify commercial-intent queries.

**Meta Ads (Facebook/Instagram)**: more effective for consumer AI products or broader B2B audiences. Less precise targeting than LinkedIn for specific business roles.

**Sponsorships**: newsletters, podcasts, and communities read by target buyers. Often more cost-effective than display advertising for niche B2B audiences.

## When paid acquisition makes sense

Paid acquisition requires:
- A clear, measurable conversion event (trial signup, booked call, purchase)
- A baseline cost-per-acquisition that the business can sustain
- Enough margin in the offer to absorb paid acquisition cost
- Proof that the offer converts from non-paid traffic first

Do not invest in paid acquisition to test whether an offer converts. Test the offer with organic outreach first. Once conversion is proven, use paid channels to scale.

## Cost-per-acquisition modeling

Before starting paid campaigns, model your maximum allowable cost per acquisition (CAC):

- Average customer lifetime value (LTV)
- Target LTV:CAC ratio (typically 3:1 to 5:1 for healthy businesses)
- Maximum CAC = LTV / target ratio

If LTV is $5,000 and target ratio is 4:1, maximum CAC is $1,250.

## Pros

- Scales predictably with budget once a conversion-positive campaign is found
- Immediate traffic, unlike organic channels which take months
- Precise targeting by role, industry, and interest

## Cons

- Expensive relative to organic channels, especially for B2B audiences
- Traffic stops when spend stops
- Requires ongoing optimization expertise
- High minimum effective budgets to generate statistically useful data

## When to use

Paid acquisition is appropriate for AI SaaS products and productized services after organic conversion is proven. It is premature for service businesses without a clear, repeatable conversion process.

## Metrics to track

- Cost per click (CPC) by channel
- Conversion rate from click to lead or trial
- Cost per qualified lead
- Cost per acquisition
- LTV:CAC ratio
