# Content Marketing

Content marketing builds awareness, credibility, and inbound leads by creating and distributing useful content that demonstrates expertise. For AI service providers, content that solves specific problems for target buyers is more effective than broad educational content.

## How to implement

1. Choose one or two content formats that fit your style and audience: blog posts, LinkedIn articles, Twitter/X threads, YouTube videos, or newsletters
2. Focus on a narrow topic area that matches your buyer's specific pain points
3. Create content that demonstrates practical knowledge — tutorials, case studies, breakdowns
4. Distribute consistently on platforms where your buyers are active
5. Track which content generates qualified inquiries and create more of that type
6. Include a clear next step in high-performing content (book a call, join a list, download a resource)

## Content types that work for AI services

- How-to guides demonstrating a specific AI workflow or implementation
- Case studies (anonymized if needed) showing real results
- Breakdowns of how to evaluate AI tools for a specific use case
- Comparison of approaches with honest pros and cons
- Explanations of real limitations and tradeoffs in AI implementations

Content that avoids hype and addresses real challenges tends to build more trust than promotional content.

## Pros

- Builds compounding authority over time
- Attracts buyers who are actively looking for solutions
- Content can drive inbound leads without ongoing paid spend
- Demonstrates expertise more credibly than claims

## Cons

- Slow to produce results — typically 3–12 months before meaningful inbound traffic
- Requires consistent production and distribution effort
- Hard to attribute revenue directly to specific content pieces
- Competitive in popular topic areas

## When to use

Content marketing is a medium-to-long-term channel appropriate for builders who can create useful, specific content consistently. It complements outbound channels by building credibility that makes outreach more effective.

## Metrics to track

- Content pieces published per month
- Inbound inquiries from content
- Most-viewed or most-shared content pieces
- Email list growth from content
- Time from first content interaction to first qualified conversation
