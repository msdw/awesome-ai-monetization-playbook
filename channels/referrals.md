# Referrals

Referral channels generate new clients or customers through recommendations from existing clients, professional contacts, or satisfied users. Referrals typically convert at higher rates than cold outreach because the recommender has transferred credibility to the provider.

## Types of referrals

**Client referrals**: satisfied clients mention your work to colleagues or contacts who have similar needs. This is the most natural and trusted referral source.

**Professional referrals**: colleagues, complementary service providers, or community members recommend you when a relevant opportunity comes up.

**Incentivized referrals**: structured programs where existing clients or users receive a benefit (discount, credit, fee) for successful referrals.

## How to generate more client referrals

1. Deliver excellent outcomes — the most reliable driver of organic referrals
2. Make it easy for clients to explain what you do — give them a clear, simple description
3. Ask directly at the right moment: after a successful delivery or at the end of a project
4. Express specific appreciation when a referral is made
5. Follow up on every referral promptly — slow follow-up wastes the referrer's credibility

## How to build a referral program

For productized services or SaaS products:
1. Define the referral benefit clearly (credit, discount, fee share)
2. Create a simple referral link or form
3. Track referral source accurately
4. Pay referral rewards promptly — delayed rewards damage participation
5. Communicate how the program works clearly to participants

## Pros

- Highest conversion rate of any acquisition channel
- Referred clients tend to have higher trust and lower friction
- Low cost per acquisition
- Compounds naturally as the client base grows

## Cons

- Cannot be manufactured directly — requires delivering genuine value first
- Slow to build as a primary channel in the early stages
- Incentivized referrals can create quality issues if the incentive attracts unqualified referrers
- Difficult to scale predictably

## When to use

Referrals should be an active component of every AI service business from the first client. They require deliberate nurturing — excellent delivery, clear communication, and direct requests at appropriate moments.

## Metrics to track

- Percentage of new clients from referral sources
- Referral conversion rate (referral introduction to paying client)
- Most productive referral sources by individual or channel type
