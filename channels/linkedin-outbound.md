# LinkedIn Outbound

LinkedIn outbound is direct outreach to potential buyers through LinkedIn connection requests, direct messages, and InMail. It is one of the most effective channels for B2B AI service providers targeting professionals at specific companies and roles.

## How to implement

1. Define a specific target buyer role (e.g., Head of Marketing at B2B SaaS companies with 20–200 employees)
2. Use LinkedIn Sales Navigator or standard search to build a list of 50–100 qualified profiles
3. Send a connection request with a short, honest note — not a sales pitch
4. After connection is accepted, send a first message that leads with a relevant observation or question
5. If there is a positive response, offer a short call to understand their situation
6. Track connection rate, reply rate, and booked call rate to measure effectiveness

## What a good outbound message looks like

- Short: 3–5 sentences maximum
- Specific to the recipient: reference their role, company, or a relevant signal
- No pitch in the first message
- Clear reason for reaching out that feels relevant, not generic
- A single, low-commitment ask (e.g., "Would it make sense to connect?")

## Pros

- Reaches decision-makers directly without advertising cost
- Can be personalized to specific industries or roles
- Builds professional relationships that compound over time

## Cons

- Response rates are typically low (5–15% for well-targeted outreach)
- Spam perception is high — most LinkedIn outreach is generic and ignored
- LinkedIn limits the number of connection requests per week
- Requires consistent effort over months before reliable pipeline builds

## When to use

LinkedIn outbound is appropriate for B2B AI service providers targeting professionals at specific company types. It is less effective for consumer products, developer tools, or self-serve SaaS.

## Metrics to track

- Connection acceptance rate (target: 30–50%)
- First message reply rate (target: 10–20% for well-targeted outreach)
- Qualified conversation rate
- Booked call rate from outreach
