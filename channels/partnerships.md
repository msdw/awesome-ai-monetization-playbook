# Partnerships

Partnerships are arrangements where two complementary parties share leads, revenue, or clients in ways that benefit both. For AI service providers, partnerships can accelerate reach without requiring advertising spend.

## Types of partnerships

**Referral partnerships**: complementary service providers refer clients to each other when they encounter needs outside their scope. A developer shop refers clients needing AI strategy; an AI strategist refers clients needing implementation.

**Technology partnerships**: integrating with or building on a platform's ecosystem gives access to that platform's user base and distribution.

**Agency partnerships**: AI specialists partnering with traditional agencies (marketing, consulting) to deliver AI capabilities to those agencies' existing clients.

**Channel partnerships**: a complementary service provider sells your productized offer to their clients on a revenue-share basis.

## How to build referral partnerships

1. Identify 10–20 service providers who serve the same buyer type but offer different services
2. Build relationships before asking for referrals — understand their work and their clients
3. Make one clear, specific referral first — demonstrate the value of the relationship
4. Agree on clear terms if compensation is involved — document it even if informal
5. Follow up on every referred client with an update

## How to pursue technology partnerships

1. Identify platforms where your target buyers already spend time
2. Research the platform's partnership program before reaching out
3. Build an integration or demonstrate complementary value before proposing a formal partnership
4. Start with a co-marketing arrangement (guest post, webinar) before a commercial deal

## Pros

- Access to existing buyer relationships without direct acquisition cost
- Credibility transfer from established partners
- Revenue can scale through multiple partner relationships simultaneously

## Cons

- Partnership development is slow — relationships take time to mature
- Revenue share reduces per-client margin
- Partner quality affects your reputation — choose partners whose work you trust
- Partner relationships require maintenance to remain productive

## When to use

Partnerships work best for AI providers who already have a working offer and are looking to expand reach. They are less effective at the earliest stage before the service is proven.

## Metrics to track

- Number of active partnership conversations per quarter
- Referrals received per partner per quarter
- Revenue attributed to partner-referred clients
- Conversion rate from partner referrals compared to direct outreach
