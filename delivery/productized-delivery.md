# Productized Delivery

Productized delivery applies a standardized, repeatable process to deliver a defined service to multiple clients consistently. The same core workflow is used for every client, with variations only in inputs and outputs.

## How to implement

1. Document the complete delivery process as an SOP before taking on more than one client
2. Create templates for all recurring deliverables (proposals, reports, communication)
3. Build an intake process that captures all required client information upfront
4. Automate the AI-assisted portions of the workflow with repeatable prompts and configurations
5. Create a quality review checklist that is completed before every delivery
6. Track time per delivery and compare to the estimate used in pricing

## SOP elements for productized delivery

A complete SOP for a productized service should include:
- Step-by-step delivery instructions
- Required inputs from the client
- Tool and template checklist
- AI prompts and parameters used
- Quality review criteria
- Delivery format and handoff process
- Revision handling process

The SOP should be detailed enough that another person could deliver the service with it.

## Scaling productized delivery

Productized delivery scales by:
- Increasing the number of concurrent clients (limited by quality attention)
- Hiring or contracting additional delivery resources with the SOP
- Automating more steps with AI tooling over time
- Raising prices as the process becomes more efficient and outcomes improve

## Quality control

For each delivery:
- Complete the quality review checklist before sending to the client
- Check for AI-generated errors, hallucinations, or inappropriate content
- Verify brand voice, accuracy, and formatting match the defined standard
- Document any exceptions or customizations for future reference

## Pros

- Delivery time decreases with repetition — margin improves without price changes
- Easier to train others to deliver than custom consulting
- Consistent quality builds reputation and referrals
- Predictable delivery effort makes scheduling and capacity planning easier

## Cons

- Requires upfront investment in documentation and tooling
- Clients may request customizations that break the productized model
- Quality demands consistent attention — automation does not eliminate human review

## When to use

Productized delivery is appropriate for any service where the core process can be repeated across clients with consistent quality. It is the foundation for scaling AI services beyond the founding individual.
