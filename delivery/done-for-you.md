# Done-for-You Delivery

Done-for-you (DFY) delivery means the provider handles the full production and delivery of an ongoing output, with the client reviewing and approving the final work. The client provides context, access, and approval — not labor.

## How to implement

1. Conduct an onboarding session to capture context: brand voice, style guidelines, target audience, formats, approval requirements
2. Create a production SOP (standard operating procedure) that documents the AI workflow, human review steps, and quality criteria
3. Deliver a sample or first batch before full commitment — establish the quality baseline
4. Set a regular delivery schedule (weekly, monthly) and stick to it
5. Create a client approval step before anything is published externally
6. Track delivery time and client satisfaction per cycle

## Production workflow

A typical DFY workflow with AI assistance:
1. Context gathering and brief preparation
2. AI generation with specific prompts and parameters
3. Human review and editing for quality, accuracy, and brand alignment
4. Draft delivery to client for approval
5. Revision round if required
6. Final delivery or publication

Human review at step 3 is non-negotiable for any output that will be published or acted on by the client.

## Pros

- High client retention when delivery quality is consistent
- Clients with high-volume recurring needs can become long-term relationships
- AI tooling reduces per-unit cost over time, improving margin

## Cons

- Operationally intensive — requires reliable delivery every cycle
- Key-person dependency if only one person knows the workflow
- Scope creep from clients who want more than the defined service

## When to use

DFY delivery is appropriate for clearly defined, recurring output types (content, reports, data enrichment) where the client has a consistent ongoing need. It requires operational maturity to maintain quality at scale.

## Quality standards

Every DFY delivery should include:
- Human review before client delivery
- Brand voice and accuracy check
- Compliance review for any regulated content
- Revision tracking and documentation
