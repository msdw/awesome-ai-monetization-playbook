# Retainer Agreement Outline

This outline provides a structure for a monthly retainer agreement for AI services. It is not legal advice. Have a lawyer review any agreement before using it with clients.

---

## Template

```
RETAINER AGREEMENT OUTLINE
============================

This outline covers the key sections of a service retainer agreement.
Each section should be completed with specific terms before use.

IMPORTANT: This is an outline only. It must be reviewed by a lawyer 
before use as a legal document.

---

## 1. Parties

Provider: [Full legal name, address, registration number if applicable]
Client: [Full legal name, address]
Effective date: [Date]

---

## 2. Services

### 2.1 Monthly services included

The Provider will deliver the following services each calendar month:

- [Service 1 — be specific: what, how much, in what format]
- [Service 2]
- [Service 3]

### 2.2 Availability and response time

The Provider will be available via [communication channel] and will 
respond to client communications within [X business hours/days].

### 2.3 Monthly deliverables

By [day of month], the Provider will deliver:
- [Deliverable 1]
- [Deliverable 2]

### 2.4 Excluded services

The following are not included in this retainer:
- [Exclusion 1]
- [Exclusion 2]
- [Exclusion 3]

Any work outside this scope requires a separate written agreement.

---

## 3. Fees and Payment

### 3.1 Monthly fee

The monthly fee for these services is $[amount] [currency].

### 3.2 Payment terms

Invoices are issued on [day] of each month and are due within [X] days.

### 3.3 Late payment

Late payments accrue interest at [X]% per month or the maximum rate 
permitted by law, whichever is lower.

### 3.4 Price adjustments

The Provider may adjust fees with [30/60/90] days written notice.

---

## 4. Term and Termination

### 4.1 Initial term

This agreement begins on [date] and continues for a minimum of [X] months.

### 4.2 Renewal

After the initial term, this agreement renews monthly unless either party 
provides written notice of termination [X] days before the renewal date.

### 4.3 Termination for cause

Either party may terminate immediately if the other materially breaches 
this agreement and fails to cure the breach within [X] days of notice.

---

## 5. Intellectual Property

### 5.1 Client ownership

Deliverables created under this retainer become client property upon 
receipt of full payment for the relevant month.

### 5.2 Provider tools and methods

The Provider retains ownership of all tools, methods, prompts, templates, 
and processes used in delivery. Deliverables do not include the underlying 
production infrastructure.

---

## 6. Confidentiality

Each party agrees not to disclose the other party's confidential information 
to third parties. This obligation survives termination for [X] years.

---

## 7. Limitations

### 7.1 No guarantee of outcomes

The Provider will deliver the defined services with professional care but 
does not guarantee specific business outcomes, revenue, rankings, 
conversions, or results from the use of deliverables.

### 7.2 AI limitations

Deliverables may include AI-generated content that has been reviewed and 
edited by the Provider. The Provider does not guarantee the complete 
accuracy of AI-generated content. The Client is responsible for final 
review before any external use.

### 7.3 Liability cap

The Provider's total liability under this agreement shall not exceed the 
fees paid in the preceding three months.

---

## 8. Signatures

Provider: _________________________ Date: _______________

Client: _________________________ Date: _______________

```

## Notes on using this template

- This outline is a starting point only. Every engagement is different.
- Regulated industries (healthcare, finance, legal) require additional compliance terms.
- The liability cap and IP terms are standard but should be reviewed by legal counsel.
- Do not sign any agreement you do not fully understand.
