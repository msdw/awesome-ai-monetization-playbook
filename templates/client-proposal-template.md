# Client Proposal Template

This template provides a structure for an AI service proposal. Adapt it to the specific engagement, client context, and offer.

A proposal should not be sent before a discovery conversation. The proposal confirms understanding and presents a solution — it is not a cold introduction.

---

## Template

```
PROPOSAL
========

[Your name / company name]
Prepared for: [Client name]
Date: [Date]
Reference: [Optional reference number]

---

## 1. Your Situation

Based on our conversation on [date], we understand:

- You are currently [describe the relevant context about their business]
- The challenge you face is [describe the problem in the client's terms]
- The impact of this problem is [time lost, cost, risk, or opportunity missed]
- You have tried [describe previous approaches if discussed]

If any of the above is incorrect, please let me know so I can revise this proposal.

---

## 2. What We Propose

We propose [name of service or package] to [describe the outcome].

### Scope of work

This engagement includes:
- [Deliverable 1]
- [Deliverable 2]
- [Deliverable 3]
- [Deliverable 4]

This engagement does not include:
- [Exclusion 1]
- [Exclusion 2]

### Timeline

Start date: [Date or "within X days of agreement"]
Delivery: [Date or duration]
Milestones: [List key milestones if applicable]

---

## 3. Our Approach

[2–3 paragraphs describing how you will approach the work. What methods, tools, or processes will you use? What makes your approach suitable for this client's situation?]

---

## 4. Your Responsibilities

For this engagement to proceed smoothly, we will need:
- [Client responsibility 1 — access, data, stakeholders, etc.]
- [Client responsibility 2]
- [Client responsibility 3]

---

## 5. Investment

| Component | Price |
|---|---|
| [Deliverable or phase] | $_____ |
| [Recurring component if applicable] | $_____ /month |
| **Total** | $_____ |

Payment terms: [50% upfront, 50% on delivery / monthly invoice / etc.]

All prices are [currency]. [Tax statement if applicable.]

---

## 6. Assumptions

This proposal is based on the following assumptions:
- [Assumption 1]
- [Assumption 2]

If any assumptions prove incorrect, the scope or price may need to be adjusted. We will notify you before proceeding with any changes.

---

## 7. Risks and Limitations

[Be honest about relevant risks specific to this engagement:]
- [Risk 1]
- [Risk 2]

---

## 8. Next Steps

To proceed:
1. Review and confirm this proposal by [date]
2. Sign the service agreement
3. Submit the initial payment of $[amount]
4. Complete the onboarding form / schedule the kickoff call

Questions? Reply to this proposal or contact me at [email].

---

*This proposal is valid until [date].*

```
