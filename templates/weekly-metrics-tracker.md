# Weekly Metrics Tracker

This template provides a structured way to track the key metrics of an AI service or product business on a weekly basis. Consistency in tracking is more valuable than the sophistication of the metrics.

## Notes on using this tracker

- Track consistently, even in slow weeks — the trends are the signal, not individual weeks
- The gross margin estimate should improve in accuracy over time as you track actual API and delivery costs
- If a metric is consistently red, investigate root cause rather than tracking the symptom
- This tracker is a decision-making tool, not a reporting obligation — adapt it to what you actually use
