# Client Onboarding Checklist

A structured onboarding process sets the right expectations, collects the information needed to deliver, and establishes the working relationship on a professional foundation.

---

## Template

```
CLIENT ONBOARDING CHECKLIST
============================

Client: ____________________
Service: ____________________
Start date: ____________________
Primary contact: ____________________

---

## Before First Session

[ ] Signed service agreement or proposal received
[ ] Upfront payment confirmed
[ ] Onboarding questionnaire sent and completed
[ ] Kickoff call scheduled
[ ] Project workspace created (shared folder, Notion, or equivalent)
[ ] Communication channel agreed (email, Slack, etc.)
[ ] Primary point of contact confirmed on both sides

---

## Onboarding Information to Collect

### About the client's business

[ ] Company overview (industry, size, what they do)
[ ] Relevant team structure and stakeholders
[ ] Key business goals this engagement supports

### About the work

[ ] Current state of the process or system we are working on
[ ] Access to relevant data, documents, or systems
  [ ] Login credentials (stored securely — not in email)
  [ ] Data export or connection details
  [ ] Relevant internal documentation or SOPs
[ ] Examples of good output (samples, past work, reference material)
[ ] Examples of bad output (what to avoid)
[ ] Brand voice, style guide, or tone guidelines (for content work)

### Operational details

[ ] Invoice recipient and billing email confirmed
[ ] Preferred communication hours and response time expectations
[ ] Preferred feedback and review process
[ ] Decision-maker for approvals identified

---

## Kickoff Call Agenda

Duration: 30–60 minutes

1. Introductions and relationship setup (5 min)
2. Review the signed scope and confirm understanding (10 min)
3. Walk through the delivery process and timeline (10 min)
4. Review what we need from the client (access, approvals) (10 min)
5. Establish communication norms (5 min)
6. Confirm next steps and immediate actions (5 min)

---

## Post-Kickoff Actions

[ ] Kickoff notes sent to client within 24 hours
[ ] Action items for client listed with deadlines
[ ] First delivery milestone confirmed in shared calendar
[ ] Client has access to shared workspace
[ ] Any missing information requests sent

---

## 30-Day Check-in

[ ] Brief check-in call or message to confirm satisfaction
[ ] Any process adjustments documented
[ ] Next milestone confirmed

```
