# Delivery Checklist

This checklist covers the standard phases of delivering an AI-powered service or implementation. Adapt it to the specific offer and client context.

---

## Template

```
DELIVERY CHECKLIST
==================

Client: ____________________
Offer / project: ____________________
Start date: ____________________
Target delivery date: ____________________

---

## Phase 1: Discovery and Setup

[ ] Discovery call completed
[ ] Discovery notes documented and shared with client
[ ] Scope confirmed in writing (proposal or SOW signed)
[ ] Upfront payment received
[ ] Kickoff call scheduled
[ ] Client access and credentials collected (data, systems, accounts)
[ ] Access reviewed for security and privacy requirements
[ ] Data handling and confidentiality terms confirmed
[ ] Project workspace created and shared with client
[ ] Timeline and milestones shared with client

---

## Phase 2: Build or Production

[ ] Requirements documented in detail
[ ] First draft or prototype built
[ ] Internal quality review completed
  [ ] AI output reviewed for accuracy and relevance
  [ ] Brand voice and tone check completed
  [ ] Factual claims verified where applicable
  [ ] No hallucinated references, statistics, or names
[ ] Draft or prototype shared with client for review
[ ] Client feedback received and documented

---

## Phase 3: Revision and Finalization

[ ] Client feedback addressed in revision
[ ] Revision round documented
[ ] Final version quality review completed
[ ] Final version approved by client (written confirmation)
[ ] Final deliverable prepared in agreed format
[ ] All required assets and files organized

---

## Phase 4: Delivery and Handoff

[ ] Final deliverable sent to client
[ ] Delivery confirmation received from client
[ ] Delivery documentation provided (user guide, runbook, or SOP)
[ ] Training session completed (if included in scope)
[ ] Access credentials transferred to client ownership (if applicable)
[ ] Monitoring configured (if included)
[ ] Known limitations and edge cases documented for client

---

## Phase 5: Post-Delivery

[ ] Invoice sent (final payment)
[ ] Payment received
[ ] Client satisfaction check (brief message or call)
[ ] Case study or testimonial requested (optional, with permission)
[ ] Retainer or next engagement discussed
[ ] Project retrospective notes saved for process improvement

---

## Compliance and Risk

[ ] Personal data handled according to agreed terms
[ ] AI outputs reviewed for bias or harmful content
[ ] Client notified of any limitations relevant to their use
[ ] Compliance notes delivered for regulated domains (if applicable)

```
