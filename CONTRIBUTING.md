# Contributing to Awesome AI Monetization Playbook

All submissions are reviewed by maintainers before merging.

## Ways to Contribute

### Suggest an offer

Open an issue using the [Suggest Offer template](https://github.com/msdw/awesome-ai-monetization-playbook/issues/new?template=suggest-offer.yml).

Good offer submissions include: what it is, who buys it, pricing signals from public sources, and delivery model.

### Suggest a resource

Open a [Suggest Resource](https://github.com/msdw/awesome-ai-monetization-playbook/issues/new?template=suggest-resource.yml) issue.

### Report a problem

Open a [Problem Report](https://github.com/msdw/awesome-ai-monetization-playbook/issues/new?template=report-problem.yml).

## Quality Bar

### What gets accepted

- Offers with observable pricing signals (cited from public sources like Upwork, Reddit, job boards, product pages)
- Pricing data clearly marked as `observed_public_price` — not invented
- Monetization models with documented real-world examples

### What gets rejected

- "Guaranteed income" or "passive income" claims
- Pricing data without sources
- Fake or invented pricing signals
- Offers in regulated domains without compliance notes
